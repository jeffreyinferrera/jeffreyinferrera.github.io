About Me

I am a graduate student at the University of Texas at Austin working towards my Master's Degree in Computer Science. I graduated from Cornell University with an undergraduate degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering and have worked as a consultant engineer for several years. My goal is to advance research in the field of intelligent robotics.
